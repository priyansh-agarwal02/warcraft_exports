# Warcraft Exports — Pricing Structure

## Retail Pricing & Orders
Warcraft Exports manufactures and sells historical reproductions directly. 
Prices are displayed dynamically in USD on individual product pages. We offer multi-currency conversion at checkout.

## Shipping Fees
Flat shipping rates are calculated based on weight and target region. We offer **Free Standard Shipping** worldwide on all orders exceeding **$150.00 USD**.

### United States / United Kingdom / Canada / Europe
- **Standard Shipping:** $14.99 USD (Free for orders over $150.00 USD)
- **Express Shipping (DHL/FedEx):** $35.00 USD
- **Est. Delivery Speeds:** Standard: 7–14 business days | Express: 5–8 business days

### Rest of the World (All Other Countries)
- **Standard Shipping:** $19.99 USD (Free for orders over $150.00 USD)
- **Express Shipping:** $39.99 USD
- **Est. Delivery Speeds:** Standard: 10–20 business days | Express: 6–10 business days

## Wholesale & Bulk Ordering
Warcraft Exports is a primary supplier to historical retail stores, museum gift shops, film prop departments, and reenactor associations.

- **Wholesale Threshold:** Orders must contain a minimum of 100 pieces (mixed or single item) to qualify for wholesale discounts.
- **Minimum Order Value (MOV):** $500.00 USD.
- **Discount Structure:** Tiered discounts ranging from 20% to 45% off retail rates depending on order volume and item complexity.
- **Custom Inquiries:** Submit bulk requirements at /wholesale or email warcraftexports@gmail.com.
